Carmandeer

  • High Concept: Avoid traffic as long as you can.
  • Player Experience Goals: Survival, dodging.
  • Genre: Aracde.
  • Game play: Navigate through the traffic and avoid getting into an accident.
  • Prototype goal: Collision detection, traffic aversion.
  • Features: Timed survival gameplay.
  • Setting/Premise: The freeway.
  • Story: Your car was just commandeered. On an infinite freeway you must avoid traffic and survive as long as you can.
  • Target Audience: Casuals.
  • Play time: Varies
  • Strategies: Anticipating the paths of the cars to make short term or long term judgements in movement.
  • Prototype Assets: I drew the cars and background myself.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Street Race.
