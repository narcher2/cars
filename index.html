Assassin!

  • High Concept: Navigate through the castle, avoiding guards, to assassinate the king.
  • Player Experience Goals: Stealth, platforming, exploration.
  • Genre: Platformer.
  • Game play: Player can move throughout the castle (using arrow keys and the spacebar), to avoid the guards, and eventually track down the king to exact revenge.
  • Prototype goal: Platforming/stealth. Multiple pathways to goal.
  • Features: There are multiple paths the player can take to find the king.
  • Setting/Premise: Time/betrayal, medieval castle.
  • Story: You were betrayed by The King of Time long ago. After plotting your revenge and tracking him down to this castle, the Time is finally here. Avoid the guards, because on contact you will be trapped in time forever (and must press F5 to restart). Search the castle for the king and exact your revenge.
  • Target Audience: Casuals.
  • Play time: Less than a minute.
  • Strategies: Navigate through shorter pathways by anticipating the paths of the guards, or sneak around the long way to avoid being detected.
  • Prototype Assets: I used the charas-RPG generator for the characters; I did the background myself.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Mario.
